# Privacy Policy — Tenth Tone

**Last updated:** 2026-05-05

This Privacy Policy describes how Tenth Tone ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Tenth Tone mobile application or website.

## Information we collect

### Account information
When you sign up, we collect your email address or phone number, and the password you choose (stored hashed, never in plain text). You may also voluntarily provide a display name, username, profile photo, and bio.

### User-generated content
We store the videos, photos, comments, messages, and other content you submit through the app.

### Location (optional)
With your explicit permission, we collect your device's GPS coordinates to show your location to friends on the in-app map. You can disable this at any time in Settings → Privacy. Your location is shared based on your visibility setting:
- **Public** — visible to all app users
- **Friends** — visible only to users you follow
- **None** — not visible to anyone (default)

### Camera and microphone
With your explicit permission, we access your device camera and microphone solely to record videos within the app. We do not record without your action and do not access these between sessions.

### Photo library
With your explicit permission, we access your photo library to let you upload existing videos and photos.

### Usage data
Standard server logs (IP address, request timestamp, user agent) are kept for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention.

## How we use information

- Provide and operate the app's features (auth, feed, messaging, live streams, maps)
- Personalize the "For You" feed via the recommendation algorithm
- Send notifications (in-app and push, only with your permission)
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## How we share information

- **With other users** — your profile and public videos are visible to other users; your DMs are only visible to recipients
- **With service providers** — we use Supabase (database, auth, storage) and Vercel (hosting). These vendors process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
- **For legal reasons** — when required by law, court order, or to protect users
- **We do not sell personal information.**

## Your rights

You have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data (Settings → Account → Delete Account, or email us)
- Export your data
- Object to processing

For requests, email **privacy@tenthtone.app**.

## Data retention

- Account data: until you delete your account
- Video content: until you delete it or your account
- Server logs: 30 days
- Backups: encrypted, 90 days

## Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We use industry-standard security practices but cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

Tenth Tone is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in EU/EEA). If we learn we have collected data from such a child, we delete it.

## International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We use standard contractual clauses where applicable.

## Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email.
